# Sketch 006: Mobile Responsive

## Design Question
How does the wizard form and timeline dashboard feel at 375px?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/006-mobile-responsive/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Bottom Nav** — Phone frame with bottom tab navigation, generate form with step indicator
- **B: Full-Width Cards** — Dashboard view with full-bleed content cards, pull-to-refresh hint
- **C: Stacked Steps + Results** — Post-generation view with platform results stacked vertically, copy/save actions

## What to Look For
- Does the form feel comfortable to fill out on a phone keyboard?
- Are the platform chips tappable enough (min 44px touch targets)?
- Does the timeline/dashboard scan well on a narrow screen?
- Is the bottom nav thumb-reachable?
- Do the copy/save actions feel natural on mobile?
- How does the content preview text truncate?
