# Sketch 015: Landing Page "How It Works" Redesign

## Design Question
How should the "How It Works" section guide visitors through the product journey?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/015-landing-how-it-works/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Vertical Timeline** — Left-aligned timeline with connecting line, numbered circles, and content cards that slide in on load. Each step has a visual hint (form, AI, clipboard). Best for storytelling flow.
- **B: Interactive Cards** — Three equal-width cards with hover animations (top border reveal, number scale). Clean grid layout with abstract visual placeholders. Best for scannable, modern feel.
- **C: Horizontal Process Flow** — Compact horizontal bar with step badges, arrows between sections, and minimal text. Best for quick comprehension and space efficiency.

## What to Look For
- **Clarity** — Can a first-time visitor immediately understand the 3-step flow?
- **Visual hierarchy** — Do the step numbers/icons draw the eye first?
- **Engagement** — Which layout makes you want to scroll further or click "Get started"?
- **Mobile feel** — How does each variant collapse on small screens?
- **Brand alignment** — Does it feel calm, professional, and approachable?
