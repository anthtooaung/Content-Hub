# Sketch 017: Image Upload Form Integration

## Design Question
How should a compact image upload button fit into the existing campaign wizard form without disrupting the flow?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/017-image-upload-form/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Compact Inline** — Small "Add image" button below the textarea with hint text. Minimal footprint, shows preview inline when image is added.
- **B: Above Campaign** — Upload area between business name and campaign textarea. Larger drop zone with preview. Image is positioned as a campaign input.
- **C: Separate Section** — Bordered section with its own header and "Optional" badge. Feels like a distinct form group. Drop zone with drag hint.

## What to Look For
- Does the upload feel natural in the form flow or like an afterthought?
- Is the preview state (image added) clear without being disruptive?
- Does the "Optional" label reduce pressure or add clutter?
- Which placement feels like the right hierarchy — image before or after campaign description?
