# Sketch 016: Social Proof / Stats Section

## Design Question
How should the landing page build credibility using real metrics and product facts instead of fake testimonials?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/016-landing-testimonials/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Big Number Cards** — Four equal cards with animated counters (3 platforms, <10s generation, 100% free, 5+ content types). Platform logos below. Clean, scannable, modern.
- **B: Inline Stats Bar** — Single horizontal bar with 4 stats separated by dividers. Compact, minimal, works as a subtle separator between sections.
- **C: Asymmetric Feature Stats** — One large hero card (blue gradient, big number) with two smaller cards stacked beside it. Tells a story: "3 platforms" is the hero, speed and free-tier are supporting facts.

## What to Look For
- **Honesty** — Do the numbers feel real and trustworthy for a hackathon project?
- **Visual weight** — Does the section feel substantial enough to anchor the landing page?
- **Mobile collapse** — How does each layout handle small screens?
- **CTA proximity** — Does the stats section naturally lead to the "Get started" button?
