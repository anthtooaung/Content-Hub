# Sketch 002: Generate Form

## Design Question
How should the campaign form and platform results be composed together?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/002-generate-form/index.html

## Variants
- **A: Side-by-Side** — Form on left, results stacked on right (desktop grid)
- **B: Tabbed Results** — Full-width form, platform tabs to switch results
- **C: Wizard Flow** — Step-by-step: form -> loading -> results

## What to Look For
- Does the form feel easy to fill out, not overwhelming?
- Are results clearly differentiated by platform?
- Is the generate action prominent and inviting?
- How does each layout handle mobile?
- Which feels most natural for a first-time user?
