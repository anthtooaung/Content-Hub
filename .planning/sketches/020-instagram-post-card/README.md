# Sketch 020: Instagram Post Card

## Design Question
What does a pixel-perfect Instagram post card look like when displaying AI-generated content with/without user-uploaded images?

## How to View
open .planning/sketches/020-instagram-post-card/index.html

## Variants
- **A: With User Image** — Full Instagram post card with gradient avatar ring, author + verified badge, square image, heart/comment/share/bookmark actions, likes count, caption with hashtags, comment input, timestamp. The complete experience.
- **B: No Image (Prompt)** — Same card structure but with an "Add an image to this post" placeholder in the square image area. Shows the empty state.
- **C: Feed Context** — The generated post shown between two other posts in a simulated Instagram feed, with story row above and a "Generated by Content Hub" badge. Shows how it fits in context.

## What to Look For
- Does the card feel like a real Instagram post or a styled approximation?
- Is the square (1:1) image ratio correct for Instagram?
- Is the "Add image" placeholder in Variant B clear without being jarring?
- Does the "Generated" badge in Variant C feel trustworthy or suspicious?
- Are the action icons, caption, and comment section proportionally correct?
